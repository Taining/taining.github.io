About

I am a tenure-track Assistant Professor in the Department of Computer Science at Loyola University Chicago. I received my Bachelor’s and PhD degrees in Computer Science from National University of Singapore, advised by Prof. Chee-Yong Chan. My research interests include databases, query processing, graph data management, data analytics, applied AI/ML, AI accountability, and formal methods.
